<section id="timeline">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Timeline:</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Jul, 1856-
        Nikola Tesla Is Born
        Tesla was born to Serbian parents in the village of Smiljan, Austrian Empire</li>
      <li>1875-Enrolls At Austrian Polytechnic</li>
      <li>1882-He begins working for Continental Edison</li>
      <li>1882-Identifies Magnetic Induction Field Principle</li>
      <li>Jun, 1884-arrives in the United States</li>
      <li>1885-quits working for Edison</li>
      <li>1886-Tesla forms Tesla Electric Light and Manufacturing Company</li>
      <li>1887-War of Currents era begins</li>
      <li>Apr,188-begins experimenting with X-Rays</li>
      <li>May,1888-Tesla Delivers Lecture: A New System of Alternating Current Motors and Transformers</li>
      <li>1891-Patents the Tesla Coil</li>
      <li>Jul, 1891-becomes a citizen of the United States</li>
      <li>1893-Nikola Tesla and George Westinghouse power the World's Columbian Exposition</li>
      <li>1899-Tesla moves to research facility in Colorado Springs</li>
      <li>1901-Construction begins on Tesla's Wardenclyffe Tower</li>
      <li>1913-Patents Bladeless Turbine</li>
      <li>Aug,1915-Tesla Files Lawsuit Against Guglielmo Marconi for stealing his nobel prize</li>
      <li>Jul,1934-The New York Times publicizes Nikola Tesla's particle Beam research</li>
      <li>Jan 7 1943-Nikola Tesla died of heart failure alone in room 3327 of the New Yorker Hotel</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
